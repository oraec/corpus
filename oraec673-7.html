Translation: A thousend (of) bread and beer, a thousand (of) bulls and fowl, a thousand (of) alabaster (vessels) and (pieces of) linen, a thousand (of) all things from which the god lives, for the ka of the dignified priest Mentuhotep, born of Hathoremhat.
