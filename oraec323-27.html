Translation: "An offering which the king gives and Osiris, lord of Abydos, the great god, Wenennefer: a thousand of bread, beer, oxen and fowl, an invocation offering at every festival, for the ka of the keeper of the diadem, the attendant of the royal palace, Nebipusenusret, conceived of (the lady) Ita."
